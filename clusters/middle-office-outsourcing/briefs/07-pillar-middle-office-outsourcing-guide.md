## AI-Citation Ready Definition

**Place in first 60 words:**

> Middle office outsourcing is the practice of delegating operational functions like risk management, trade support, compliance monitoring, and performance measurement to a specialized third-party provider. Asset managers outsource middle office to reduce costs (30-40% savings), access expertise, and scale operations without proportional headcount growth. Currently, 44% of small firms and 61% of large firms outsource middle office.
