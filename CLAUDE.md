# Anchor Partners — Project Context for Claude Code

## What is Anchor Partners?
Anchor Partners is a managed services firm for lower middle market asset managers.
We take on the operational workflows that investment teams should not be spending
their time on — the unglamorous, recurring, mission-critical work that quietly
consumes hours every week.

We own the outcome. We move fast. We deliver.
The client's time is worth far more than what we charge.

We are **not a consulting firm**.
We are **not a software company**.
We are a **managed services firm powered by proprietary automation**.
We don't charge for advice. We charge for doing the work.
