### H2: FAQ (300 words)

5-6 questions, 50-60 words each:

1. **What is the best investment operations outsourcing provider?**
   The best provider depends on AUM, service scope, and urgency. Pure-play specialists serve $50M-$2B firms needing fast implementation. Traditional administrators serve $2B+ firms needing comprehensive services. Evaluate based on your specific requirements.

2. **How much do providers cost?**
   Pure-play providers: $20K-$100K/year (subscription). Traditional fund administrators: Custom, often asset-based. Technology platforms: $10K-$50K/year (software only). Compare to $85K-$120K per in-house FTE.

3. **How long does onboarding take?**
   Pure-play specialists: 4-8 weeks. Traditional administrators: 12-20 weeks. Technology platforms: 2-4 weeks (software only). Timeline depends on complexity and integrations.

4. **Are providers secure?**
   Yes. Institutional providers maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance, bank-grade encryption, and professional liability insurance.

5. **Can you switch providers?**
   Yes. Subscription-based providers typically offer flexible contracts. Review termination clauses and data portability before signing.
