- The symptom: NAV errors, incorrect client statements
- The cost: Reputation, investor confidence
- Real-world consequence examples
- What help looks like

#### H3: 3. Regulatory Deadlines Cause Panic (150 words)
- The symptom: Last-minute scrambles for Form PF, 13F, etc.
- The cost: Compliance risk, staff burnout
- The threshold: If you've missed or nearly missed a deadline
- What help looks like

#### H3: 4. You're Failing Operational Due Diligence (100 words)
- The symptom: Allocator questions you can't answer
- The cost: Lost capital raises

- What help looks like

#### H3: 5. Key Person Risk is Too High (100 words)
- The symptom: One person knows everything
- The cost: Business continuity risk
- The threshold: If vacation = panic
- What help looks like

#### H3: 6. You Can't Scale Without Hiring (100 words)
- The symptom: Each new fund/strategy requires headcount
- The cost: Margin compression
- The threshold: Ops cost growing faster than AUM
- What help looks like

#### H3: 7. Your Technology is Spreadsheets (100 words)
- The symptom: Critical processes in Excel
- The cost: Version control, audit trail gaps
- The threshold: If "the spreadsheet" is mission-critical
- What help looks like
